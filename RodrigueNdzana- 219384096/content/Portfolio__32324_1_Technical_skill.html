Technical skill

Situation:

I recall a specific instance during which I undertook a project focusing on the management and monitoring of available vehicles within an organizational setting. These vehicles serve as a cornerstone for the day-to-day operations of the organization. However, the existing system utilized to track these vehicles was notably ineffective, resulting in frequent misunderstandings and sporadic unavailability of vehicles. This inefficiency directly impacted the productivity and smooth functioning of the operations.

Task:

The task at hand involves implementing a more efficient and streamlined system to monitor the availability and usage of vehicles. The primary goal is to create a database or system that enables real-time tracking, ensuring vehicles are efficiently allocated, reducing downtime and enhancing overall operational efficiency.

Action: I was tasked of developing a vehicle tracking system within the project. Recognizing the inefficiencies in the current method, I conducted a thorough analysis of the existing system and identified the main issues i realised it was due to lack of a centralized database, manual record-keeping, and inadequate means to track real-time vehicle usage and availability.

To address the issue, I designed and implemented a modern, user-friendly digital platform for tracking available vehicles. Using my technical expertise, I selected a suitable software solution that would facilitate real-time updates and data entry. This platform integrated GPS tracking systems installed in the vehicles to provide live location data.

 I developed a comprehensive SQL database that stored information about each vehicle, including its unique identification(Primary key), maintenance history, current location, and availability status. This database was linked to the centralized system, allowing authorized personnel to access real-time information.

 To streamline the process further, I automated alerts for scheduled maintenance, enabling proactive servicing. Additionally, I set up automated notifications for low fuel levels or upcoming renewals for registrations and insurance, minimizing the risk of unforeseen issues impacting vehicle availability.

Result: As  a result the new system significantly enhanced operational efficiency by ensuring timely vehicle allocation, minimizing downtime, and reducing confusion about vehicle availability. The availability of real-time data facilitated better decision-making regarding the allocation and utilization of the vehicles. The proactive maintenance and automated notifications resulted in cost savings by avoiding unexpected repair costs and improving the overall lifespan of the vehicles.
